HELMET

Probably the most important tool you’ll need! Wiping out occasionally is unavoidable (especially if you’re new) and just like any activity that has the potential for your head to impact a hard surface, proper protection is recommended. You won’t need a helmet specific to your riding preference or ability but you do have options.

An in-mold helmet will be lighter and sleeker, while an injection-mold helmet will offer a greater amount of shock absorption and durability. We also recommend getting your helmet sized to fit your head. Your helmet should fit snugly (but not too tight).

There are other cool features you can explore like camera mounts and built in audio.
